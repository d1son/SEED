Student / Employee Database

As a employer you will be able to view potential student/employee information such as their GitHub, stackoverflow, Facebook, Linkedin, Twitter,portfolio and personal skills and strength. Obtain the prospective student/employees contact information to reach out.

Employer Database

As a student/employee you will be able to view company information and also the reviews and ratings powered by Linkedin and Glassdoor. You will also be able to view companies that are currently looking for and hiring skilled web developers just like you!
